About Nikita

With over 5 years of experience, Nikita Dakua brings creativity and precision to every look. From natural daytime makeup to dramatic evening transformations, she tailors her services to your unique style. Based in Shaktifarm (Udham Singh Nagar), she travels for events and shoots.
